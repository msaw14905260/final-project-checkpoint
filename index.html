Final Project Proposal

Project Title: Worldwide Gender Inequality

Project Overview

Dataset: World Bank Gender Data

In this project, we aim to analyze global gender disparities across education, labor, and health indicators using a multi-year cross-country dataset. Our goal is to uncover regional patterns, long-term trends, and structural inequalities that affect women and girls worldwide. We will explore relationships between metrics such as literacy rates, labor force participation, adolescent fertility, and children’s employment. Using descriptive analysis and multi-variable comparisons, we will highlight where progress has been made and where gaps persist.
